Regenerating Self-Signed Citadel Certificates

Dec 5th, 2011

From citadel.org:

service webcit stop
service citadel stop
cd /etc/ssl
rm -Rf citadel/* webcit/*
export O="Your Organization"
export OU="Your Server"
export CN="*"
service citadel start
service webcit start
